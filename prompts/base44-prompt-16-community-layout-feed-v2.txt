## Goals

1. Feed content uses available width inside the wide shell.
2. Quick Actions rail stops duplicating sidebar navigation.
3. Community tabs (Feed / Teams / Messages / People) share one consistent layout grid.

---

## Layout spec

### Desktop (≥1024px)

```txt
Outer: max-w-6xl or max-w-7xl or max-w-[1920px] (keep current wide shell)
Grid:  [ main feed column ]  [ optional discovery rail ]
```

Main feed column:

```txt
min-width: 0
flex: 1
max-width: none OR max-w-3xl/4xl inside the grid — NOT fixed 680px
```

Target: primary readable column **≥60%** of viewport on 1440px (≈860px+).

Right rail (optional, collapsible):

- Suggested coaches
- My teams snippet
- **Remove** Quick Actions that duplicate sidebar: Find a coach, Go to teams, My trainees (already in nav)

Keep rail only for **discovery** content not in sidebar.

### Mobile

- Single column
- Composer accessible (FAB or inline — keep current mobile behavior)

---

## Feed tab specific

Remove or hide on desktop:

```txt
QUICK ACTIONS
Find a coach
Go to teams
My trainees
```

These are redundant with left sidebar: Find Coach, Trainees, Teams.

Keep:

- Composer (inline or top)
- Following / Discover filters
- Feed posts
